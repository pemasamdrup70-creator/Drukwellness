Our Story & Mission

The Bhutanese community in Perth has grown significantly over the past decade, with many individuals and families facing unique challenges related to migration, acculturation, and adjustment to Australian society.

Although local community associations provide general support, these initiatives are largely informal and lack a structured professional framework for mental health and counselling.

Druk Wellness was created to bridge this gap by establishing a digital platform that connects Bhutanese counsellors in Perth, promotes supervision and collaboration, and increases access to culturally responsive counselling for the Bhutanese community.
